Part-1: Training & Practice

In this phase, parents play a crucial role by conducting regular practice tests for students. These tests can be scheduled weekly, daily, or in accordance with guidelines provided by the school. This consistent practice ensures that students build a solid foundation in each chapter.

Testing & Competitions

After completing the training phase, students will undergo a one-time chapter-wise quiz to assess their understanding. In addition, there will be grand quiz exams and opportunities to participate in national-level competitions to further challenge and enhance their skills.
