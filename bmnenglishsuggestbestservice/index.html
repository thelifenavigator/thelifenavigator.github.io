Psychological Services Questionnaire for Household Members

This questionnaire is designed to help you determine which type of psychological service is best suited for your situation. Please answer the following questions honestly to receive the most accurate recommendation.





1. How would you describe the current atmosphere at home?




2. How do you feel about your relationship with your spouse/partner?





3. How do you feel about your relationship with your children?





4. How do you feel about your relationship with your parents or in-laws?





5. How do you feel about your relationship with other relatives?





6. How do you perceive your child's behavior at home?




7. How do you perceive your child's behavior at school or with peers?




8. Have you noticed any changes in your child's mood or behavior recently?




9. How often do you feel stressed or overwhelmed?




10. How would you rate your current level of anxiety or depression?





11. Do you have someone to talk to about your feelings and concerns?





12. How do you feel about your ability to manage daily responsibilities and tasks?




13. Are you open to seeking professional psychological help?




14. How often do you feel tense or anxious when you think about what others might be saying about you??





15. How would you prefer to attend counseling sessions?




16. If a family member is experiencing behavioral issues, which situation best describes it?





17. If you are experiencing marital problems, which situation best describes it?





18. If an elderly family member needs support, which situation best describes it?
